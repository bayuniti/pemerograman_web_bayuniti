About Hotel Royal Singapore

Hotel Royal Singapore is a 4-Star smoke-free hotel in Singapore with 331 well-appointed guest rooms and suites.

We are strategically located right in the heart of Singapore’s most exclusive district and only minutes away from the renowned shopping and entertainment haven of Orchard Road. The Newton MRT Station is within a minute walking distance from the hotel. Public transport such as buses and taxis are also easily available from the hotel’s main entrance to bring guests to all parts of Singapore.

Guests who stay in the 331 luxurious guest rooms and suites of the hotel will experience a sense of "Every Room A Home" due to the comfortable decor of pastel colours, background, and soothingly coloured wallpaper. Our rooms are also highly respected for their generous sizes in Singapore.

For guests’ convenience & entertainment, Wi-Fi access is available, and extra additional satellite and cable TV channels linked to all guest rooms & suites, apart from the hotel’s present 24 hours in-house movie channels.

The hotel’s 24 hours Trishaw Coffeehouse offers mouth-watering local delicacies and irresistible desserts. Room service is also available 24 hours. Happy Joy Chinese Restaurant and Halal Japanese Restaurant offer daily lunch and dinner and are fabulous places to dine in comfort.

Various seminar packages are available and the conference & seminar rooms are well equipped to cater both large and small groups, be it informal seminars or just for hosting parties.

There is an in-house gym for the health-conscious and also a self-service laundrette & ironing room for guests who prefer to do their own laundry whilst staying in the hotel.

Please come to stay in Hotel Royal and experience the Traditional Royal Service that we offer.

ISO 9000

ISO 9001:2000 specifies requirements for a quality management system for any organization that needs to demonstrate its ability to consistently provide products and services that meet customer and applicable regulatory requirements and aims to enhance customer satisfaction.

Compliance with ISO 9001 demonstrates our organization takes quality and customer satisfaction seriously. Our organization was awarded ISO 9002:1994 in the year 1997. We have converted to ISO 9001 in year 2002.

Hotel Security Award

The National Crime Prevention Council (NCPC) of Singapore is a non-profit organization committed to promoting public awareness of and concern about crime and to propagate the concept of self-help in crime prevention.

Since its inception in 1981, the Council had actively engaged the public through the organization of crime prevention activities. Working closely with the Public Affairs Department of the Singapore Police Force, the Council holds the annual festive season crime prevention campaigns, as well as regular exhibitions, seminars, workshops and talks on crime prevention.

Hotel Royal has been working in close association with the National Crime Prevention Council and has been awarded several gold awards over the past years since 1997 for excellent security practices.

Arts Supporter Award

Incepted in September 1987, the National Arts Council (NAC) is a statutory board under the Ministry of Information, Communications and the Arts of Singapore. Their mission is to nurture the arts and make it an integral part of life in Singapore.

Awards are presented yearly to artists for artistic excellence and to patrons for contributing to the promotion and development of the arts in Singapore.

Hotel Royal has been a strong supporter for the Arts and has been awarded for Arts Supporter Award in 2014.
